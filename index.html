Unveiling Talent, Celebrating Culture

Sounds of Morocco is a 100% independent label from Morocco, supporting Moroccan DJs and producers as well as international talent. More than a label, it’s a cultural project led by producer Yas.Weeyzz, celebrating music, creativity, and the rich Moroccan heritage through innovative productions and artistic collaborations while helping artists release their music worldwide.
