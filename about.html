About the Label

“Sounds of Morocco is an independent label and distribution platform dedicated to promoting talent globally. We support artists through production, distribution, creative direction, and cultural projects.”

  • Production & creative direction
  • Global distribution to major stores
  • Artist services (branding, strategy, press)
  • Residencies & workshops

Cultural Project

“The Sounds of Morocco Cultural Project explores Moroccan roots and modernity through music, residencies, collaborations, and artistic research.”

Led by Yas.Weeyzz, this initiative connects traditional timbres with modern electronic production, building a living archive of sound and performance.
